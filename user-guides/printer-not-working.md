# Hardware-Printer Not Working - Troubleshooting Guide

## Problem
Printer does not print or is unresponsive.

## Possible Causes
- Printer not connected
- Paper jam
- Low ink/toner
- Driver issues
- Offline printer status

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Check power and cable connection
2. Ensure printer is turned on
3. Clear any paper jams
4. Check ink/toner levels
5. Restart printer and computer
6. Set printer as default device

## Advanced Fixes
- Reinstall printer drivers
- Remove and re-add printer
- Check print spooler service

## Resolution
Most issues are fixed by restarting and reinstalling drivers.

## Prevention Tips
- Keep drivers updated
- Regular maintenance cleaning
- Use quality paper and ink
